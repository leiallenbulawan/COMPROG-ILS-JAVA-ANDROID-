Meet the Programmers

This project was developed by ICT-MAWD-111 students as part of a programming project. The team designed CollabNotes to improve collaboration and productivity.

Where This Project Was Created

The project was created at STI College Dasmariñas as part of our ICT course.

Why We Made This App

We created this application to help students and teams collaborate and manage notes efficiently. Users can upload and share their notes for others, creating a community of students.
